Ideation Tool

Mind Map Instructions

  • Click "Add Idea" to create nodes
  • Drag nodes to organize them
  • Click "Connect" on a node, then click another node to link them
  • Double-click text to edit
  • Click "Delete" to remove a node

Canvas Instructions

  • Select a color from the palette
  • Click "Pen" to draw freely
  • Click "Add Text" then click on canvas to add text
  • Click "Eraser" to erase
  • Use "Clear All" to start fresh
